    Hostel 7
    Hostel 7 was inaugrated on 8th December 2006 by Ms Sushma Berlia.
    It has the capacity of 150 students. It has good facilities regarding infrastructure. It's infrastructure include beautifully maintained garden, professional gym, Music Room, TV room, badminton court, table tennis room, library and a suitable study room. They are very well maintained. Every corner of the hostel has a washroom in every floor. These washrooms are very clean. The staffs make sure that these washrooms are cleaned everyday. Each wwashroom has a washing machine. The washing machine work properly. Every room has the facility of LAN. The LAN provide high speed internet to the student which helps them to learn smoothly using internet. If any hinderence takes place, they are fixed very soon by the staffs. It also have a guest room, which are provided to the student's relative.

    Hostel 7E
    Hostel 7E is the extension of Hostel 7. The extension was started on september 2014. It has the common facilities just like Hostel 7. All the infrastructure like gym, music room, Tv room,etc can use by the students of Hostel 7E. Hostel 7 and 7E has common mess, which serves them a good quality food. The mess is very well maintained by the Mess staff. We could definitely say that our Mess serves the best food in NITJ. Just like Hostel 7, Hostel 7E has the LAN facility in every room. It provide them high speed internet. The washroom of Hostel 7E is much advance version. The washrooms have both the Indian and Western facilities. It also have a guest room, which are provided to the student's relative. It has water cooler at every floor. The cooler is attached to a purifier, which provide the students healthy drinking water.
