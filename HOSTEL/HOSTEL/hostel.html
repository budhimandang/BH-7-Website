    Description

    Hostel 7 & 7E are among the best Hostel in NIT Jalandhar. It is just neighbour to Hostel 6 and Mega Hostel B-Block. It's location is far away from the crowd, which keep the hostel environment very calm. It has various facilities which other hostel lacks in NITJ. Those facilities are described below in infrastructure-section.
    Hostel 7 & 7E have the best mess. It's among the highly rated mess. It serves a very good quality food. The Menu of the mess are decided by the Mess-Committee itself. The Committee ensures that every month the menu changes so that the student dont have a monotonous taste.
    These Hostel also have a very good staff member. They are very helpful. They maintained the garden, keeps the hostel environment clean.

    Infrastructure

    It has good facilities regarding infrastructure. It's infrastructure include beautifully maintained garden, Professional Gym, Music Room, TV room, Badminton court, Table Tennis Room, Library and a suitable Study Room. They are very well maintained.
