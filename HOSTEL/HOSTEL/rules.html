    Hostel Rules

    1. Ragging is strictly prohibited. If someone is caught, strict action will be taken.
    2. Students should enter hostel before 12pm. If someone is found outside the hostel after 12pm, they have to pay fine.
    3. All the facilities like Gym is only for Hostel 7 & 7E students. Students from other hostel is not allowed. If they are found then they have to pay Rs 5000/- as a fine.
    4. Mess facility is mandatory for all inmates.
    5.Institute does not own the responsibility of theft and loss of any items possessed by the inmates. However, in the interest of inmates they may insure their valuables.
    6.Possessing any kind of motorized vehicle, e.g. car, scooter, motorcycle, moped etc. by the hostlers is not permitted.
    7.All the boarders shall vacate the hostel rooms before they leave for vacation so that annual repairs and white washing etc. may be carried out. All the hostel articles issued to the students should be returned to the Hostel Office before they leave the hostel. They will be responsible for the loss, if any, and which will be recovered from them.
    8.The students shall make proper use of common rooms having newspapers, allied literature and other articles required for indoor games. The hostel warden will fix hours of the common room from time to time.
